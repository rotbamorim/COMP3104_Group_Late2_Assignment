The Software Development Life Cycle is important knowledge for any software engineer, including DevOps engineers. The SDLC is a plan for the development of a software. It is divided into Planning, Analysis, Design, Implementation, Testing, and Maintenance. It is important to learn these aspects of software development, as they will come up both in interviews and in the job. DevOps is important for the SDLC, as it allows many different people to work on a project without interfering with each other.
