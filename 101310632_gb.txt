George Brown College is a college in Toronto, Ontario. It has 3 main campuses at different locations around the city, each of which specializes in different programs. The college, also abbreviated as GBC, serves thousands of students, some of which are from Ontario, while many others are international students. GBC has a wide amount of different programs available to fit many career needs. The college features many clubs, a Student Association that fights for the rights of students, and many in campus facilities and shops. GBC is open for in person classes again as of the Fall of 2022, and COVID-19 related safety protocols are highly recommended.
