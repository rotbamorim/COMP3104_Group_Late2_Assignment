George Brown College (also abbreviated as GBC) is a college in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It is a provincially recognised college with 3 campuses in different locations in Toronto, each with a focus on different programs. GBC features programs across construction, computer science, hospitality, and many other careers. The college has many facilities and clubs in its campuses, as well as a Student Association that fights for the rights of students. The college welcomes both local and international students. The campuses have reopened for in person classes as of the Fall of 2022, with mask wearing and social distancing still being recommended.
